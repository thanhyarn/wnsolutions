Frequently Asked Questions

RFID stands for Radio Frequency Identification, a technology for identifying and collecting data about objects through radio waves. It includes key components like RFID tags, readers, and management software to automate operations and management.

Implementing RFID solutions brings several benefits such as improved operational efficiency, reduced human errors, enhanced accuracy of information, and decreased search time for goods.

RFID is widely used in warehouse management, logistics, access control, asset tracking, inventory management, and supply chain traceability.

To select the right RFID solution, businesses should clearly define their specific needs including scale, usage purposes, operational environment, and technical requirements. Consulting with experts and conducting trials before deployment are crucial to ensure effectiveness and suitability of the solution.
